About STARKIT

Starkit is an interactive website that helps children discover their unique personalities through fun quizzes and friendly fox characters.

Specially created for high-functioning autistic children aged 10 to 12, Starkit utilizes gentle stories, colors, and visual design to help
children understand their strengths, emotions, and ways of thinking in a safe and encouraging environment.

Each fox represents a personality type from the DISC model (Dominant, Influential, Steady, Conscientious) — Ruby, Sunny, Willy and
Snowy. These friendly foxes guide children to see that everyone learns and grows differently.

Starkit invites both children and parents to explore together. Through the quiz, character stories, and "Star Identity Card," children can build
confidence while parents gain deeper insight into their child's personality.

Starkit's core is not just a test, but a journey of understanding, acceptance, and growth, allowing every child to shine in their own way.

What is High-Functioning Autism?

Understanding high-functioning autism helps parents recognize their child's unique ways of learning, feeling, and growing.

High-functioning autism refers to children on the autism spectrum who demonstrate average or above-average intelligence and language abilities. While they can learn and communicate effectively, they may still face challenges in understanding emotions or social interactions.

These children often prefer routines and familiar environments that make them feel safe and in control. Sudden changes or emotionally charged situations can sometimes cause confusion or anxiety.

At the same time, they show remarkable strengths such as strong focus, creativity, honesty, and deep curiosity. They often notice small details that others might overlook and feel passionate about topics that truly interest them.

Between the ages of 10 and 12, children begin to explore who they are and how they connect with the world. For those with high-functioning autism, this stage can be exciting yet challenging, as they develop a stronger sense of self and learn to understand others.

Starkit was created to help these children understand themselves in a calm, creative, and structured way. Through interactive quizzes and friendly fox characters, the website gently guides them to explore their emotions, strengths, and ways of thinking while building confidence and self-expression.

For parents, Starkit offers a meaningful way to understand their child's personality and emotional world. It encourages communication, empathy, and shared growth, reminding families that every child shines in their own special way.

Why Focus on Children Aged 10 to 12?

Ages 10 to 12 represent a meaningful stage of growth where children start to ask, "Who am I?" and become more aware of how others see them. It is a time filled with curiosity, self-reflection, and sensitivity to social feedback.

A Message to Parents

Every child is a small universe, full of feelings, curiosity, and endless potential. Some shine brightly and confidently, while others grow quietly in their own rhythm. No matter the pace, each child's light is equally precious.

Starkit's design philosophy is to make self-discovery a gentle and joyful journey, helping children and parents better understand each other through stories, imagination, and care.

We hope this journey helps you see your child not only for who they are today, but for who they are becoming. Take time to listen, celebrate every effort, and walk beside them with patience and love.
